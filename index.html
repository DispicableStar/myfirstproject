This blog is about some national parks of India

1. Mahananda Wildlife Sanctuary, West Bengal

Located on the foothills of the Himalayas, the Mahananda Wildlife Sanctuart is considered a birdwatcher's paradise and houses endangereb species like the rufous-nacked hornbill (Aceros nipalensis).

2. Desert National Park, Rajasthan

The Desert National Park comprises of arid swathes of sand and rock characteristic of the Thar Desert.
Interspersed with spare grasslands, this habitat supportsvthe Indian gazelle(Gazella gazella bennettii) or chinkara.
